About Bill Wise

Bill Wise is an AI-powered invoice information extraction system that automatically identifies and extracts key fields from invoice images — whether they are clean scans or real photos taken with a phone.

It combines Tesseract OCR for text extraction with a custom-trained spaCy Named Entity Recognition (NER) model to locate and label structured fields directly on the document.

What it extracts

  • ID — Invoice number
  • DATE — Date of issue
  • SN — Seller name
  • CN — Client name
  • IBAN — Seller bank IBAN
  • TOTAL — Grand total amount

How it works

  1. Photo is taken with a phone and passed to the document scanner
  2. The scanner detects the document boundary using HSV white masking and contour detection
  3. Perspective warp is applied to flatten and straighten the document
  4. Brightness and contrast are adjusted to improve OCR quality
  5. Tesseract OCR extracts all tokens with their bounding box coordinates
  6. The spaCy NER model identifies and labels the key entities
  7. Results are drawn on the image with colored bounding boxes

Model Performance

  • F1-Score: ~91%
  • Precision: ~91%
  • Recall: ~91%
  • Trained on 265 invoices, evaluated on 35 held-out images

Known Limitations

When the invoice is photographed against a bright or white surface, the document boundary detection can fail since the paper and background have similar color. A YOLO-based detection model is being explored as a future solution.
