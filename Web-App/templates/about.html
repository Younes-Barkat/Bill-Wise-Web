02
HOW IT WORKS
  1. Photo is taken with a phone and uploaded to the app
  2. The scanner detects the document boundary using HSV white masking and contour detection
  3. The 4 detected corner points are shown — you can drag to adjust them manually
  4. Perspective warp is applied to flatten and straighten the document
  5. Brightness and contrast are enhanced to improve OCR quality
  6. Tesseract OCR extracts all tokens with their bounding box coordinates
  7. The spaCy NER model identifies and labels the 6 entity types
  8. Results are rendered on the image with colored bounding boxes

05
KNOWN LIMITATIONS

The system works best when the invoice is photographed against a dark or contrasting background.

White background issue — When the invoice is photographed against a bright or white surface, the HSV white mask captures both the paper and the background, making the 4-point contour detection fail. A YOLO-based object detection model is being explored as a future solution.
